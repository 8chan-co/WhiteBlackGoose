My values

Hi. I'm WhiteBlackGoose, and here is what I think is right and wrong. These values are what possible in today's world.



I'm pro...

  • Individualism
  • Right for gender self-identification. Your gender does not limited you at anything - thus you're free to choose.
  • Right for nationality self-identification. Nobody is born "Swedish" (or any other nationality) - you can only become one, by learning its culture and agreeing with its values.
  • Right for culture self-identification. You don't have to be born in a cultural environment to be part of it.
  • Right for self-expression. Nobody is a "weirdo".
  • Expansion of definition of marriage to any two adults, regardless of their self-identities.
  • Free migration. Give everyone a chance to live a good life.
  • Free movement. You can move work/study/live to another city or country without barriers.
  • Free speech. Everyone has a right to be heard, unless the message contradicts previous points or human rights.

I'm against...

  • Abuse of free speech. Free speech is when everyone has a right to contribute to the society. Any xenophobia does not contribute to it
  • Aggressive war, regardless of anything. All conflicts must be solved by the World together.
  • Militarisation. Countries should be defended by common unions without overwhelming military-wise neighbors.
  • Any kinds of xenophobia. Even if majority of a social group thinks one way - you cannot attribute this opinion to every member of this social group
  • Collective guilt. You have no right to blame someone for not opposing their social group, if they didn't choose it



WIP

These values are, however, limited by today's world. In fact, my ideals stretch far beyond these values.
